About Starfire

Starfire is a cooperative card game inspired by the fast-paced starfighter combat in sci-fi movies and TV shows. Players work together to destroy the enemy base before their squad or home base is wiped out.

With Starfire's unique "fray" system, enemy fighters pursue a specific player or the home base. Players cannot attack their own pursuers, and cannot attack the enemy base unless they are free of pursuers. This ensures that players must work together and choose their actions wisely to succeed.

Teamwork is essential to victory, but one pilot may shine above the rest. Prove your skills to your commanding officers and you may get promoted... if you survive.
